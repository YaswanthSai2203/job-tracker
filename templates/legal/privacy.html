Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026. This is a general template; adjust for your deployment.

Information we collect

When you create an account, we store your email address and a hashed password. When you use the application tracker, we store the details you enter (such as company names, links, notes, deadlines) and filenames for uploaded resumes.

How we use it

We use this information to provide the service: authentication, saving your applications, and (for administrators) publishing public job listings.

Cookies and sessions

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. In production we configure cookies to be sent only over HTTPS where applicable.

Data retention

Data is kept until you delete it or close your account, subject to your host’s backup and retention policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the operator of this deployment.
